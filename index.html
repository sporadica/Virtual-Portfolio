I'm

Designer, Marketer, Writer, Freelancer

i'm a digital media and e-learning specialist based in utah with a love for progressive design, gamified training programs, and the oxford comma.

As a writer, I wholeheartedly believe in the power of written word. Long before digital media, written literature has given us powerful insight into humanity's past and allowed us to share our dreams of fantastical futures. Throughout my work in digital communication, writing, and learning development, this belief has only gotten stronger.

Through my writing, I hope to give my readers information, perspective, and connection. As a dreamer and a writer, you will often find my nose in a book. If you can't find me there then I'm hiking with my faithful dog-panion, Yuki, drawing on my iPad, or playing an action-packed video game (or two).
